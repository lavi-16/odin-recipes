Mama's Best Lasagna

This is a recipe for lasagna. It is a classic italian dish that is made with layers of lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and marinara sauce.

This recipe is simple to makes and takes about 1hr. The ingredients are as follows:

  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Steps to make:

  1. Cut veggies
  2. Oil in pan
  3. Garlic and onions in pan
  4. add sauce
  5. cook meat
